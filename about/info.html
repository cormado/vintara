What is vintara.xyz?

Vintara.xyz is a website where you can play simple games to avoid boredom in classes, meetings or other boring situations where you don't have a good device to play on.


Who can publish a game?

Anyone can publish a game on vintara.xyz You can join our discord and open a ticket to request to add your game in our website, it is necessary that the game is made only with html, css and javascript code, you need to have a name, a synopsis and a logo for the game.

How i can support Vintara?

You can support Vintara by sharing our website with your friends, disabling your adblocker and joining the discord to have a bigger community
